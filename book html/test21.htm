I am water.

I am simmering in a kettle now.

After a while, I can go out of the kettle.

When I become vapor and go out of the kettle people call me steam.

"Wow, I am rising!" I become lighter as I get out of the kettle.

"I will rise to sky quickly."

When I rise to the sky, people call me a cloud.

"Hi, friends?" When I go up to the sky, I can meet my friends.

We always go together and watch the world.

The more friends who gather, the heavier our body becomes.

Then, we fall down to the land.

While getting down to the land, if we meet cold air, we become snow.

And if we meet warm air, we become rain.

I can be vapor, a cloud, snow or rain like this.

I am a magician.
