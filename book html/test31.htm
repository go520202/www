A long, long time ago, a traveler was walking in the path to a mountain.

"whoosh, whoosh."

He heard a strange sound and watched out carefully.

A big snake was about to eat a pheasant.

The traveler quickly shot his bow toward the big snake and the pheasant.

The day drew to a close.

The traveler slept in an open shed near an isolated house.

The sleeping traveler opened his eyes because he felt heavy.

"Oh, My God!"

A big snake was winding around the traveler's whole body tightly and darted its long tongue in and out.

"Did you kill my older brother? I am the younger brother of the big snake that you killed this afternoon."

The traveler asked the snake to let him live.

"OK, I'll let you go if the bell rings three times in the empty temple in the mountain before the sun rises."

With tears in his eyes, the traveler thought that he was going to be dead.

Day break was getting closer.

The snake wound around the traveler's body more strongly.

At that moment,

"Ding, dong, ding." the bell rang three times.

The big snake disappeared.

'What happened?'

The traveler went to the empty temple, and found the bleeding pheasant under the bell.
