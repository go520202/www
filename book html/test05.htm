A bear is catching a crayfish in the valley.

A crafty fox approaches him on tiptoe.

¡°Hey, bear, what about taking honey from the tree and sharing it?¡±

The bear follows the fox with big strides.

The fox thinks cleverly.

"Hey, bear, go up the tree, pick a hive and throw it to me. Then I will catch it."

The bear goes up on the tree secretly.
