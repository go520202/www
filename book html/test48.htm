There lived a big oriental oak in the heart of the mountain.

The oriental oak had thick bark, long leaves, and a rich mind.

One day, collared dove family came to see him.

"Oriental oak uncle, please let our family live here."

"Come, please do so. Live here happily."

The oriental oak gave a single branch to collared dove family.

The collared dove family built a house, so branch shook extremely.

But the oriental oak did not say any thing.

One day, a great spotted woodpecker, which has a sharp-pointed beak, came to say,

"Please, let me live here."

Hearing this, the collared dove said,

"No. uncle, the great spotted woodpecker is noisy because he pecks the tree."

"I can not sleep late, if he makes noise early in the morning."

But, the cork oak welcomed the great spotted woodpecker.

After sane tine, the cork oak began to suffer illness.

The collared dove family left the cork oak.

"Uncle, Where are you hurt?",the great spotted woodpecker asked.

"Bad worms entered my body and I got a disease.

Don't worry about me and leave.",the cork oak spoke feebly.

"But I cannot leave you alone. You helped me when I didn't have a place to live."

I will catch the bad worms in uncle's body."

The great spotted woodpecker clutched the cork oak with sharp toenails.

And he began to catch the worms moving his neck.

"Da da da ........."

The great spotted woodpecker pecked continually for a few days.

His neck was aching, but he did not stop catching the worms.

The sound of pecking spread far, far away.
