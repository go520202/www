The animals living in the forest gathered together, in order to discuss who would be a watchman for the village.

At first, a giraffe said,

"My neck is longer than any other animals in this forest!"

"Since my neck is long, I can see far away best.

So, I will be a watchman who keeps the village safe ...."

A horse bursted in on giraffe's speaking and said,

"No giraffe, you are too slow.

I am good at running. When a dangerous accident happens, I can inform you the fastest.

So, I must be a watchman who keeps our village safe."

An eagle listening the story without a word came forward and said,

"Since I can fly in the sky, I'm faster than a horse and I can see farther than giraffe.

So, I must be a watchman."

"Yes, that's right. The eagle can see farther than me.", giraffe nodded and said.

Suddenly, the horse said in a loud voice.

"No, I must be a watchman."
