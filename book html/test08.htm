One day, the king of animals country gave earth to a pig, a rabbit, and a deer and said,

"Children, this is marvelous earth that can treat painful injury.

Will you turn this marvelous earth into a bowl?

I'll give a prize to someone who makes the most beautiful bowl."

The animals began to make a bowl hard the next day.

And after completing their bowls, they took it to the king.

Other animals also went there to see who would win the award.

The King examined the bowls carefully.

Then, he noded his head examining deer's bowl.

A baby squirrel saw this, laughed and said,

"Ha. ha. ha. The bottom of the bowl is open." Everyone laughed some more.

"Deer, why did you make a bowl with a hole in the bottom?"

"King, I wanted to help my friend." 

Deer drooped his head and answered.

Then, a goat came forward and said,

"King, I couldn't move for fifteen days because of my injured leg.

Deer heard this news and came to me with the bottom of the bowl which he was making.

And he applied the earth to my wounded leg.

That's why the deer's bowl has a hole in the bottom."

Listening to the goat, the King was very pleased.

And he gave the deer a big reward.
