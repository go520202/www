A cat picked a mouse every day.

Mom mouse opened the family council.

The first mouse said,

"We had better move to another place. There may not be a cat in that neighborhood."

Next, the second mouse said,

"Isn't it tough to prepare for a move?  We'd rather watch out for the cat in shift."
