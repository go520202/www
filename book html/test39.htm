One afternoon, mom and daddy went to weed the field, and Doli was left at the house alone.

"Ah, I am bored." Doli opened a rabbit hutch door.

The rabbits got excited and hopped out.

When he removed the stable crossbar, cattles also ran and jumped excitedly.

The rabbits went hopping to a cabbage field.

And the rabbits munched tasty cabbages.

"No, return quickly!!" Doli ran to the cabbage field in a hurry.

"Moo, moo ." The cattle went running to the barley field.

And the cattle munched and mumbled barley.

"Oh, my god! You are not supposed to do so!" Doli stamped his foot with worries.

" Just because I was bored....." Doli wore a tearful face.
