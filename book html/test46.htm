A Jindoggae named white-dog lived with his grandmother in Jindo, Jeollanam-do.

White-dog followed his grandmother well.

Grandmother also loved white-dog.

One day, grandmother got sick.

Grandmother sold white-dog to make money to buy medicine.

White-dog was sold to a distant place, called Daejeon.

White-dog was not happy even if his new master treated him well.

He only wanted to see grandmother.

One day, white-dog left to find grandmother.

During the journey, white-dog was nearly hit by a car and fell down on the street because of hunger.

It had been seven months since white-dog left grandmother.

Sleeping grandmother was surprised at the sound of scratching at the door.

When she opened door, white-dog was standing in front of the door, looking thin and feeble.

Grandmother hugged white-dog strongly.

(He walked around 300km for seven months.)
