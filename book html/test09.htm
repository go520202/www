Mee-Young left home early in the morning.

'I will go to school earlier than my teacher and surprise him today'

Mee-Young opened classroom door.

By the way, teacher had come already.

'Aw, teacher came already.' Mee-Young was distressed.

By the way, the teacher did not see Mee-Young.

Mee-Young approached the teacher backward walking on tiptoe.

"Sir!"

"Oh, my!"

The teacher was so surprised.

"Sir, what were you doing?"

"I was talking with the goldfish. This fellow is weak. That's because we didn't change the water of a fish basin for a long time."

At that time, a good idea came to Mee-Young.

'Yes, I will change the water in the fish basin.' As soon as she finished the class, she ran to the water supply.

And she poured water into the bucket with city water in full and returned into the classroom.

Mee-Young replaced the water in the fish basin with the city water.

The goldfish struggled with being surprised.

Next morning, Mee-Young entered the classroom a little later than the other day.

Friends gathered around the fish basin and were making noise.

A friend of hers spoke in a troubled voice, "Sir, goldfish looks like crestfallen."

The teacher said "Because someone replaced the water in the fish basin with city water. Who put the city water in goldfish basin?" and Mee-Young was blushing.

Mee-Young was likely to burst into tears.

Mee-Young came back her seat and got down on her desk.

At that time,

someone put his hand on Mee-Young's shoulder gently.

It was sir.

"Mee-Young, you replaced the water for the goldfish, didn't you?

Mee-Young, teacher knows your mind well.

If you want to use city water for the fish basin from now on, put the city water in the bucket for 2~3days, and then put the water into the basin."

The teacher held Mee-Young's hands tight.
