One day, in the early daybreak, the Great King Sejong was looking around the royal palace.

He saw a young scholar who was sleeping and sitting on the desk after studying until daybreak.

"It is cold in the daybreak......." The Great King Sejong took off the clothes that he was wearing.

And he covered the young scholar with his clothes.

The Great King Sejong looked after ministers like himself.

The Great King Sejong observed how the people lived.

'How do the people live?' Sometimes he went out of the royal palace, and examined the difficulties.

And he worked hard for the people.

When the Great King Sejong ruled over the country, our writing used Chinese characters.

Chinese characters were difficult for the people to learn.

The Great King Sejong felt sorry for the people who had difficulty  learning Chinese characters.

'We need easy letters. I have to make letters which are easy for the people to learn and write.'

The Great King Sejong worked night and day to make letters that the people could write easily.

Several scholars helped the Great King Sejong.

When the Great King Sejong had an eye disease, he still did not rest and continued to make the letters.

Hangul that we use today is the letters that the Great King Sejong and several scholars made.
