As an ant walked on the street, he got caught in the rain.

The ant grabbed grass to prevent being washed out by the rain.

The ant looked around to find a place to avoid the rain.

A snail sat in rainwater, without shaking.

The ant envied the snail.

Then, a cluster mushroom called the ant from the other side.

"Ant, come here. Please come and avoid the rain."

The ant slowly approached the cluster mushroom.

The cluster mushroom kept the ant from the rain. It was like a big umbrella.

"Thanks. I am so light that I can drift away even if it rains a little.

I wish I had a house on my back like the snail."

The cluster mushroom comforted the ant.

But, the ant still envied the snail.

A few days later, The ant visited the cluster mushroom with his family.

The cluster mushroom hid the ant family from the hot sunlight.

The ant family heartly ate prepared food sharing with the cluster mushroom.

Then, The snail came in sweating.

The ant saw the snail and gave him water.

"Ant, thanks. I envy you because you are light.

I have difficulty carrying this house on my back." ,the snail said.

"Really? Frankly speaking, I envied you. But, you envied me, too!" , the ant said.

The cluster mushroom smiled gently.
