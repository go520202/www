After school, I went home, but mother was not there.

While I was alone at home, my aunt phoned.

"Is this Yoon-Ji? Is your mother there?"

"Hello? She is not here."

"I called because tomorrow is your mother's birthday..."

If my aunt didn't tell me, I would have forgotten mother's birthday.

After thinking about which gift to give her, I decided to write a letter.

When I was about to write a letter, mother came home.

As soon as mother put down a shopping basket, she made the evening meal.

After having dinner, my family had a joyful conversation.

After the conversation ended, I went to my room and wrote the letter.

I tried not to sleep, and made a color paper ring.

The next morning, I put the letter and the color paper ring on the dressing table.

My heart beat quickly

Mother was happy to see my letter and the color paper ring.

Then she tried on the color paper ring on her finger.

"Really pretty!" When I saw her pleased, I was also pleased.
