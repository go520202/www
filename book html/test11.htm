'Clang-clang, clang'

'Cuckoo, cuckoo, cuckoo .' A wall clock and a cuckoo clock were proud of their sounds.

By the way, the cuckoo clock thought its own voice was muffled because of the wall clock's sound.

The cuckoo clock was distressed.

"Hey, wall clock, why do you make a noise?", the cuckoo clock asked.

"Why do you mimic a cuckoo even though you aren't a cuckoo?" When the cuckoo clock heard this, he got angry.

'Clang-clang, clang-clang''Cuckoo, cuckoo, cuckoo, cuckoo .' It became four o'clock.

Song Yi opened the door and came into the living room.

"I have to hurry up to meet Yeong Soo. Thank you for informing me of the time."

While looking at the wall clock and the cuckoo clock, Song Yi said.

'Thank you for informing time? Song Yi is pleased because we inform her what time it is!'

The wall clock and the cuckoo clock realized that informing time exactly was more important than boasting their sounds.

And they regretted getting angry at each other.

"Wall clock, I'm really sorry to be angry at you because of making a noise.", the cuckoo clock said.

"Yes, so am I.

I didn't know that informing exact time was more important than boasting my sound.", the wall clock said.

Then, the cuckoo clock noded hearing this.

It was five o'clock.

'Clang-clang, clang-clang, clang'

'Cuckoo, cuckoo, cuckoo, cuckoo, cuckoo .', the wall clock and the cuckoo clock informed time looking at each other.
