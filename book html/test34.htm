Yun-ho has many toys.

He has played with the toys since he was a child.

In the afternoon his cousin, Yeong-ho, came to play.

"Hey, brother, Where are the toys?"

As soon as Yeong-ho enters the room, he looked for Yun-ho's toys.

Yun-ho finds the toys.

Yun-ho takes the toys out and gives the toys to Yeong-ho.

Yeong-ho plays happily with the toys.

It is time for Yeong-ho to go back to his home.

Yeong-ho says to Yun-ho.

"Brother, give me this airplane."

"No, it is my favorite toy."

"You have many other toys aside from this one, don't you?"

Yun-ho hesitates on what to do.
