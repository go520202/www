Long time ago, there were various kinds of games that children enjoyed.

Among them, let's check how to play the catching tail game, hitting a stone game and stepping shadow game.

In the catching a tail game, the front person in a line tries to catch a person who is in the tail of the same line.

When we play this game, several people stand in a line .

And the person in the back holds a front person's waist and bends his/her body.

The first person becomes a tagger and he catches a person who stands at the end of the line.

The person who is caught to the tagger becomes the next tagger.

In the hitting a stone game, you let the other person's stone fall down.

First, prepare a palm sized stone.

And toss for it to divide teams.

The team that loses in the toss puts their stones in a row.

Then, the winning team throws their stone to hit the other team's stone to make it fall down.

At this time, you should not use your hands.

The team that makes the other team's stones fall down first wins the game.

The stepping shadow game is a game in which you step on the other people's shadows.

First, toss for it to decide a tagger.

If the tagger steps on a person's shadow, he wins in the game.

If a person's shadow is stepped on by the tagger, that person becomes the next tagger.
