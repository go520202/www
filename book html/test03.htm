While Cheol-Su was watching TV in the evening.

He saw that traffic accidents happened more this year than last year.

Cheol-su wondered why traffic accidents occurred more in our country.

So, he thought about how to prevent traffic accidents.

He thought that in order to reduce traffic accidents, pedestrians had to observe traffic signals,

and cross at the crosswalk.

When he went to school, he had to cross the main road. At that time, he sometimes saw people who cross the street hastily even through a red light.

Every time he saw that kind of person, he was afraid of the situation.

So, he wanted to argue that pedestrians must observe traffic signal in order to reduce traffic accidents.
