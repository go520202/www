One day, a waterfowl saw a carp floating in a stream.

The waterfowl quickly approached the carp, then bit and raised the carp.

Suddenly, a grasshopper rushed out of the stomach of the carp.

"Oh, my, it's so hot! I felt like dying."

"Well, what were you doing in the stomach of the carp?", the waterfowl asked with laugher.

"Don't laugh. I did catch this carp, anyway."

"What? Did you say that you caught the carp that I caught?", the waterfowl became angry and said.

"Well, after I found this carp, I approached him to catch him, but, he quickly swallowed me.

But, I endured until the carp became tired."

"Oh! I saved you from the stomach of this fish, but what did you say?"

"I found this carp first?"
