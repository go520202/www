Arang went hunting.

Arang saw a rabbit and chased it quickly.

Then, the rabbit hid in a cave.

Arang also went into the cave to get the rabbit.

After walking in the darkness for a long time, he saw a glimmer far away.

He went out of the cave toward the light and was surprised.

Because that place was a totally different world from the place he had lived in.

There were houses higher than mountains and many cars.

He couldn't see animals.

Arang walked along the street, completely befuddled.

He kept walking and saw only unfamiliar things.

Arang became afraid of the situation.

He wanted to go home.

"How can I get to the cave?"

All the people pointed to a direction sign.

But, Arang could not read the direction sign.
