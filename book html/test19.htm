It is a warm spring day.

A puppy and a rabbit are seesawing under a big tree.

"Yahoo! It's exciting."

"Rabbit, jump higher!"

A pig watched beside them.

Afterward, the pig and the rabbit seesawed.

As soon as the pig stamped his foot noisily on the board,

the rabbit flew up and got caught on a branch.

"Oh dear!, what can we do?" Everyone worried about the rabbit.

At the right moment, a giraffe passing by looked at this situation and ran to them.

The giraffe made a slide using his long neck.

The rabbit slid giraffe's neck and got to the ground.

All the animals were pleased.

The giraffe also were proud of helping his friend.
