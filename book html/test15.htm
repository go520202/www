Yeong-ho layed down to sleep.

However, Dolphins that he has seen in the story book in the afternoon flashed through his mind.

The picture of dolphin's swimming was very nice.

Yeong-ho laughed with a gentle smile.

By the way, something strange happened.

The lovely dolphin which was in the story book approached Yeong-ho.

"Yeong-ho, why don't you go the sea with me sea for sightseeing?"

"OK!" Yeong-ho said and rode the dolphin to the sea.

Pretty fish waved their tails.

The blue water looked like it was dancing.

At that time, a baby fish approached Yeong-ho quickly.

"Help me. My friends are locked in the cave."

Yeong-ho went to the cave along with the baby fish.

Big stones were blocking the entrance of the cave.

Yeong-ho could not move the stones by himself.

"Yeong-ho, let's whistle to call my friends."  the dolphin said.

Yeong-ho whistled. Then, dolphins appeared.

The dolphins cooperated to clear the stones that blocked the entrance of the cave.

Finally, they could see inside the cave.

Then, baby fish poured out of the cave.

Yeong-ho cheered joyfully.

"Yeong-ho, it's time to get up."

Mother shook and awakened Yeong-ho.

"Hail to the dolphins!"

Yeong-ho cheered.

"It was a dream!"

Mother laughed loudly.

"Well, where are all the dolphins?"

Yeong-ho looked around inside his room.
