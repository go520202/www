Yeong-hui was painting in the classroom.

Hyeon-hui touched Yeong-hui's shoulder by mistake.

"You spoiled my picture!", Yeong-hui angrily said.

Since Yeong-hui got angry, Hyeon-hui didn't want to apologize.

Yeong-hui hated Hyeon-hui because she did not apologize.

So, she drew lines carelessly on Hyeon-hui drawing paper with blue pastel crayon.

"I didn't mean it! How come you drew lines on my drawing paper!", Hyeon-hui said with a tearful face.

" Hyeon-hui and Yeong-hui, what happened?", teacher asked.

" Yeong-hui drew lines carelessly on my drawing paper."

" Hyeon-hui spoiled my picture."

Teacher said with a gentle smile,

"Yeong-hui's picture became nicer because of Hyeon-hui.

And Hyeon-hui's picture became a cool sea because of Yeong-hui."

'Did it look nicer?'

'Did it Become a cool sea?'

Children crowded in to see their paintings.
