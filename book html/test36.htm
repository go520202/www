It was cold winter.

An albino puppy pooped in the roadside.

A sparrow flew down and pecked the puppy dung.

"Dung! Dung! Oh, dirty." The sparrow muttered and flew far away.

"Am I dirty?" Puppy dung was very hurt.

Earth lump sitting next to the puppy dung giggled.

"Why are you laughing?" Puppy dung cried because got angry.

"The reason why I laughed is that you are puppy dung, the dirtiest dung among the dung."

"Boo-hoos." Puppy dung bursted into tears.

Then, an oxcart came and stopped beside the earth lump.

"Did I drop the earth lump yesterday? I will get it back."

Uncle picked up the earth lump only.

Puppy dung was left alone.

As a day grew dark, white snow dropped with soft steps.

Puppy dung began to hibernate for a long time.

The warm spring came.

Puppy dung awoke from hibernation.

A bud of the dandelion sprouted up in front of puppy dung.

"I am dandelion that blows a pretty flower like a star."

"How can you blow such a flower?"

"If you become fertilizer, I can blow a pretty flower.",dandelion bud whispered with twinkling eyes.

"Really?" Puppy dung was surprised.

'I can become fertilizer!' Puppy dung was so glad that he hugged dandelion bud tight.

Spring rain dropped.

Puppy dung exposed to rain in the whole body was broken into pieces and entered the ground.

And it became fertilizer for the dandelion.

One day, sunshine was glaring, the dandelion blew a beautiful flower.

Flower smell spread by spring wind.

Dandelion blossom was filled with beautiful mind of puppy dung.
