Once upon a time, there lived a young man in a village.

The young man wanted to know how human beings was different from other animals.

So, he decided to visit a famous scholar living in his neighborhood and asked him the question.

The Young man asked,

"What is different between human beings and other animals?"

The scholar answered,

"Humans walk with two legs, unlike other animals."

But, the young man was still curious.

The next day, the young man visited the scholar while holding a rooster.

"Then, this rooster walks with two legs. Is this rooster human also?"

"Humans walk with two legs, but we don't have wings."

Still, The young man didn't think that was a perfect answer.

The next day, the young man visited the scholar along with a gorilla.

"This gorilla walks two legs and has no wings. Is this gorilla human also?"
