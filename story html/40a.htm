<세종대왕>

어느 날, 이른 새벽이었습니다. 세종대왕이 궁궐을 돌아보고 있었습니다.

One day, in the early daybreak, the Great King Sejong was looking around the royal palace.

그러다가 새벽까지 공부를 하다가 앉은 채 잠든 젊을 학자를 보았습니다.

He saw a young scholar who was sleeping and sitting on the desk after studying until daybreak.

"새벽이라 추울 텐데......."  세종대왕은 입고 있던 옷을 벗었습니다.

"It is cold in the daybreak......." The Great King Sejong took off the clothes that he was wearing.
