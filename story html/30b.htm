이방은 겨울이라서 산딸기가 없다고 대답하였습니다.

The petty official answered that there were no wild berries because it was winter.

그러자 원님은 산딸기를 따오지 않으면 벌을 주겠다고 하였습니다.

Thereupon, the county magistrate told him that he would punish him if he did not get the wild berries.

겁이 많은 이방은 그만 병이 나서 눕고 말았습니다.

The Cowardly petty official was sick in his bed.

이방에게는 영리한 아들이 있었습니다.

He had a smart son.
